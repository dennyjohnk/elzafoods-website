THE POISON IN OUR DAILY DIET

In the American, European and eastern countries, there are comprehensive laws for the sale and slaughter of public fish and meat products, its preservation and the quality of the fish and meat sold to public. The slaughter houses are governed and only the highest quality of processed fish and meat products that have been frozen and processed are sold to the public. This is because large numbers of human diseases are caused by eating contaminated fish and meat.


FISH

In India, dead fish are stored in boats for days and weeks and then stored unscientifically in ice blocks mixed with ammonia and many other dangerous chemicals.

The habit of people in Kerala is to buy fish convinced to the lies of Fish Traders about Fresh Fish “that they brought from the harbor in the morning”. Think how many Fish Traders can deliver genuinely good fish to the public when the "fresh" fish stored in the ice can be bought for less than half of what it sells in the morning on the same day evening. Even if there is a law that requires all Fish Traders to buy fish in the morning itself, the real issue is that we do not have so much quantity of Fish available from the sea.

Let’s talk about the quality of Fish from a Fish Traders who bought it at a high price in the morning itself from the harbour. He must keep it in the ice box for at least six to seven hours. The outside temperature of a fish, stored in ice boxes is a minimum of plus 10 degrees Celsius to plus 20 degrees Celsius. Therefore, the freshness of the fish stored in the ice decreases with each passing moment, and over this long period of time we get decayed fish that is sold to us labeled as "Fresh" which is actually decomposed fish that is unclean, dirty and completely unhealthy.


MEAT

Beef with minimal blood and fat is good for body strength, vitality and immunity.

When our ancestors produced edible meat, they slaughtered the animals and cut them into four parts, seasoned them with turmeric, pepper, coconut oil and a small amount of indium and then hung them for hours, leaving as much as fat and blood as waste.

The average buffalo used for normal meat in our country has about 20-22 liters of blood. That is, about 10 percent of the total weight of an average cow contains blood.

In contrast, the modern-day approach of beef consumption is very dangerous. Nowadays, beef is prepared by tying the nerves in the neck immediately after the slaughter without removing as much blood as possible. It is also a common practice to cut the the buffalo into four pieces and then spread the blood back into it again using the hands of the butchers. Meat prepared in this way is infiltrated in large quantities by bacteria and viruses. This also doubles the decomposing of the dead cow. Not only that, the practice of killing a buffalo by beheading it with hoe is completely unscientific. The buffaloes that die in this way, the hormone of fear are released into the blood. Eating this blood-stained meat can spread to humans and cause diseases such as mad cow disease or Creutzfeldt-Jacob disease (vCJD).


WHERE IS THE HEALTHY FOOD?

So, in a place like Kerala where 99% of the people are carnivores, who generally get only unhealthy fish and meat, is there no healthy fish and meat diet?

YES. BUT IT IS EATEN ONLY BY PEOPLE OUTSIDE INDIA.

Our own Arabian Sea coast is one of the tastiest fishing grounds in the world. Our export quality seafood is sold at high prices in foreign countries. Professional cutting of fish is done immediately; tunnel freezing is completed at a temperature of minus 40 degree Celsius and stored in deep freezers at a temperature of 20 degree Celsius.

India is the world's fourth largest exporter of fish and the second largest exporter of meat in the world. This is due to the high quality, taste and health of our processed food with advanced technology. When frozen at minus 40 degrees Celsius, even the core temperature inside the fish and meat products drops to temperatures ranging from minus 25 degree Celsius to minus degree 30 Celsius. Once processed, the bacteria are completely destroyed. Fish and Meat Processing turns even the cells and tissues of Fish and Meat to freeze resulting in Cellular level freezing that result in complete stoppage of Decomposing process of Fish and Meat. In addition, the nutrients and flavor are locked and firmly established in frozen foods.

While our frozen food is eaten in large quantities all over the world, we are consuming dangerous fish and meat stored at unhealthy temperatures of plus 20 degrees Celsius full of dangerous and disease causing germs and bacteria.
